# Settings Module

## Purpose
This module facilitates the manage of settings within the system. It allows for the tracking, reporting, and classification of critical business records.

## Form Fields & Controls
- **COMPANY NAME**: [text] - Primary record identifier for classification.
- **BRANCH NAME**: [text] - Primary record identifier for classification.
- **CURRENCY SYMBOL**: [text] - Captures standardized information for records.
- **TAX RATE (%)**: [number] - Captures standardized information for records.
- **TAX CALCULATED ON**: [select] - Standardized categorization dropdown.
- **SHOW PURCHASE PRICE ON INVOICE**: [select] - Standardized categorization dropdown.
- **NEW PASSWORD (MIN 8 CHARACTERS, LEAVE BLANK TO KEEP CURRENT)**: [password] - Captures standardized information for records.
