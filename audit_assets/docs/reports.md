# Reports Module

## Purpose
This module facilitates the manage of reports within the system. It allows for the tracking, reporting, and classification of critical business records.

## Form Fields & Controls
- **FROM DATE**: [date] - Chronological tracking for historical reporting.
- **TO DATE**: [date] - Chronological tracking for historical reporting.
- **YEAR**: [number] - Captures standardized information for records.
- **MONTH**: [select] - Standardized categorization dropdown.
